\chapter{Dataset and Quality Filtering Details}
\label{app:dataset}

This appendix provides quantitative and visual summaries of the final
EMIT--Sentinel-2 training dataset introduced in Section~\ref{sec:qc}.
Section~\ref{app:pair-stats} reports the acquisition statistics of the
110 scene pairs that survived quality filtering. Section~\ref{app:qc-attrition}
breaks down the per-filter tile attrition at the regression-based QC
stage. Section~\ref{app:qc-gallery} presents representative tile
examples for each quality category.


\section{Scene-pair statistics}
\label{app:pair-stats}

Table~\ref{tab:pairs-summary} summarises the 110 EMIT--Sentinel-2 scene
pairs in the working dataset. The median absolute time gap between an
EMIT acquisition and its Sentinel-2 counterpart is 24.1\,h, and 82\% of
all pairs fall within a 48-hour window. Tables~\ref{tab:pairs-landcover}
and~\ref{tab:pairs-year} break the pairs down by AOI land-cover class
and by EMIT acquisition year, respectively.

\section{Quality-filter attrition}
\label{app:qc-attrition}

Table~\ref{tab:qc-filters} reports the number of tiles rejected by each
regression-based quality filter, relative to the 12{,}043 tiles that
entered the QC stage. The filters are not mutually exclusive, so a tile
may fail more than one check; the per-row counts therefore sum to
slightly more than the unique total. Counts of tiles removed earlier by
the Sentinel-2 SCL cloud screening during tile extraction were not
preserved in the QC log and are not included here.

\begin{table}[h]
  \centering
  \begin{tabular}{lrr}
    \toprule
    QC filter & tiles failed & \% of total \\
    \midrule
    EMIT cloud contamination                       & 649    & 5.4\% \\
    S2 reverse regression $R^2 < 0.70$             & 1{,}666 & 13.8\% \\
    Forward regression $R^2 < 0.75$                & 1{,}296 & 10.8\% \\
    \midrule
    \textbf{Total rejected}                        & \textbf{3{,}494} & \textbf{29.0\%} \\
    \textbf{Total retained}                        & \textbf{8{,}549} & \textbf{71.0\%} \\
    \bottomrule
  \end{tabular}
  \caption{Per-filter tile attrition at the regression-based QC stage.}
  \label{tab:qc-filters}
\end{table}
